Design Principles

Armando Kamisaki

Alignment

CNN International

The page shows horizontal alignment, which brings good readability to the users. The elements are well organized and easy to navigate.

Contrast

Amazon Aid Foundation

There are color contrasts that help the users see 3 main purposes: the green picture with a jungle, meaning the nature. The message in white color, about the world climate. And the Donate botton in orange, asking for our donation.

White Space and Clean Design

American Printing House for the Blind

The black and white colors help the users understand the company's purpose. It is clean, and easy to read and navigate. The black and white looks simple, and, at the same time, honest and easier to understand the message.
