Target Audience

Description

Our visitors are people living in Southeast Idaho who wants to know the weather conditions, singles traveling in groups looking for natural landscape, and families looking for places to visit in Southeast Idaho.

Persona 1

James Brown – 45 years old, married and has 2 children. He is a business director, works for many hours everyday. He wants to take his family for a vacation. He is looking for a good place to stay and rest for the weekend, so he searches the place and the local weather. He uses his desktop at work.

Persona 2

Mary Smith – 25 years old, single, nurse, lives alone. She loves to be with people. She gets alone with everybody at the hospital. She wants to travel with her friends to a new place, where she can meet new people and have fun. She looks for the weather and check if the weather is good for hiking or climbing the mountains. She uses her mobile device.

Scenarios

Scenario 1 – Visitor lives in Southeast Idaho area, so he searches for the weather report

Scenario 2 – Visitor wants to visit Soda Springs, so he looks for the city’s weather conditions, and informations about the place.

Scenario 3 – Visitor wants to visit Preston, so he looks for the city’s weather conditions, and informations about the place.

Scenario 4 – Visitor wants to visit Fish Haven, so he looks for the city’s weather conditions, and informations about the place.

Scenario 5 – Visitor wants a place to feel the nature. He or she looks for the weather and information about Southeast Idaho.
