Fishing Activity

We’re fond of this particular hobby because it’s great for all ages and skill levels. It’s a wonderful way to enjoy nature, spend time with friends and family members, and unwind from the daily grind.

The practice of fishing—also known as angling—began over 40,000 years ago as a means of nourishment. Fish were caught in both freshwater and saltwater locations, and anglers counted on their daily catch for survival. These days, anglers fish for a multitude of reasons. So, why does fishing reel so many people in? There are at least five reasons:

1) It’s a great stress reliever; 2) It’s good for physical and mental health; 3) It promotes bonding - fishing is a great activity for families and groups of friends; 4) It’s fun; 5) Anyone can do it.
